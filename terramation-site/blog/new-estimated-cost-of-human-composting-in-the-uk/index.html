New estimated cost of human composting in the UK

More and more people are interested in human composting in the UK as a sustainable funeral option.

This week a new article published in the Mirror in the UK revealed what the estimated price of human composting would be in the UK if it were made legal. 

You can read the article here: https://www.mirror.co.uk/news/weird-news/inside-human-composting-newest-way-32491784At Terramation UK we know thousands of our readers are interested in a more sustainable funeral choice, and the idea of leaving no trace of our remains appeals to many. 

As for the estimated cost of human composting in the UK, the Mirror article has said that it could cost just over £5,000 for the process. This is taken from the current cost of the process offered by US services mentioned in the article like recomposed. 

What do you think of this price? Would you want to pay this amount for human composting?
