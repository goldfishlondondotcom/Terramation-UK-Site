About sustainable funeral options

As terramation is not yet available in the UK, the most eco-friendly options currently available are natural and woodland burials, and water cremation (aquamation), which became legal in Scotland in March 2026.

Natural burials avoid embalming chemicals and non-biodegradable coffins, allowing the body to decompose naturally. Woodland burials can actively support local biodiversity and conservation. Water cremation uses alkaline hydrolysis — a process that uses around 90% less energy than cremation and produces no direct carbon emissions.
