# Advertising Outreach Sequence

Use these as starter emails for corporate B2B outreach to relevant sustainable funeral providers.

## Email 1: introduction

Subject ideas:

- Reach readers already researching sustainable funerals in the UK
- Advertising opportunity on Terramation UK

Body:

```text
Hi {{ contact.FIRSTNAME | default:"there" }},

I’m reaching out from Terramation UK.

We run a content and campaign platform focused on human composting, sustainable funerals, and greener end-of-life choices in the UK. We have recently launched a provider-facing directory page for readers actively researching lower-impact funeral options.

I thought {{ contact.COMPANY | default:"your organisation" }} could be a strong fit for a featured placement.

We’re currently offering:
- Featured Provider placements
- Category sponsorships
- Sponsored content bundles

If useful, I can send over the current options and placements.

Best,
Joe
Terramation UK
https://terramation.uk/advertise/

If this is not relevant, just reply and I won’t follow up.
```
