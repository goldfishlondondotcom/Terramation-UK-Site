# Ad Sales Process

## Core offer

Start with three products:

- Featured Provider
- Category Sponsorship
- Sponsored Content Bundle

## Pipeline stages

Track every lead in one of these stages:

- `lead_found`
- `qualified`
- `contacted`
- `interested`
- `proposal_sent`
- `won`
- `not_now`
- `do_not_contact`

## Weekly workflow

1. Add 20 to 40 relevant providers to the lead sheet.
2. Classify each as `corporate`, `sole_trader`, or `unknown`.
3. Send outreach only to the corporate batch.
4. Review clicks, replies, and enquiries.
5. Move warm leads into proposal and follow-up.
6. Stop all outreach for anyone who objects.

## Best-fit advertisers

- Natural burial grounds
- Eco-focused funeral directors
- Woodland burial providers
- Water cremation / resomation businesses
- Memorial woodland and tree planting services
- Relevant aligned charities or organisations

## Sales assets

- `/advertise/` landing page
- `/find-sustainable-funerals/` directory
- outreach sequence
- lead CSV import template
- simple media kit PDF or deck

## Manual checks before outreach

- Is the contact clearly a business contact?
- Is the business genuinely relevant to sustainable funerals?
- Is the source URL saved?
- Would this placement make sense to a reader?
